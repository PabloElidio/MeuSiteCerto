League of Legends season 2023: Is it worth playing the game?

by PotatoTomato
January 1, 2023
Share on FacebookShare on Twitter

League of Legends is a game that was introduced in 2009 and has gone through various alterations and development procedures since then. As 2022 concludes and 2023 begins, the issue of whether League of Legends is still a game worth putting in emerges.

ADVERTISEMENT

League of Legends and the creators behind it

The MOBA title has evolved significantly since its first release. The devs altered the gameplay, meta, champions, fundamental quality of life elements, and numerous other aspects.

What makes League of Legends so popular is that it has possibly one of the most active creators of any other esports product. Riot Games responds to the fanbase faster than any other company in the industry.

Yes, there are times when specific buffs and nerfs take a long time to apply. However, issues are handled in a much broader context later on. For example, Yuumi was a hot issue for a long time, and fans continued screaming that she required modifications, which in the end, she did.

This is just one example of many as League of Legends continues to receive overhaul, VGU upgrades, ASU updates, changes, adjustments, and so much more virtually every month.

The champion designs in League of Legends are also among the most distinctive ever. The strength of these champions might be overwhelming at times, but they are quite inventive in terms of design, demonstrating how much work the creators put in.

It’s also worth noting that, with Riot Games aiming for League in 2023, this is likely the most beginner-friendly MOBA game on the market. Dota 2 is also available, but it is far too hard for a rookie player to grasp. The overhaul of Yuumi, the release of Ixal enchanter, the jungle recommendations in preseason, and many more, all aimed at making League more and more friendly-based.

Finally, Riot goes out of their way to look after their professional players. At the pro level, the League of Legends community is incredibly well-developed, with every player’s present and future assured. 

As a result, if players desire a quick answer, the answer is yes. It is worthwhile to participate in 2023 with League of Legends. Although this may not satisfy everyone, the game will always be worth playing since players will never feel excluded and will always find a massive community that grows from time to time.
